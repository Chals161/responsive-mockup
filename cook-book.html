Cookbook

For my cookbook typography project, I embarked on a heartfelt journey to capture the essence of a cherished family recipe, one that had been lovingly passed down through generations of my family. This culinary treasure is none other than the fragrant and flavorful Indian comfort food, biryani. With each fragrant bite, this dish brings forth memories of my grandmother, mother, and now, my own experiences in the kitchen. I wanted to create a typography design that would not only pay homage to this beloved recipe but also evoke the warmth and nostalgia associated with it.

Selecting the right fonts was crucial to achieving this goal. I carefully curated a combination of typefaces that would complement the narrative and recipe while providing a unique visual identity. For the title font, I opted for Nocturne Serif Medium. Its distinctive tail added a touch of elegance and flair, harmoniously connecting with the ascenders and descenders of the typeface. This choice set the stage for the book.

To maintain a balance between the title and subheading, I turned to Questa Grande Medium. This font exudes simplicity while maintaining an enchanting charm, thanks to its captivating ball terminals and break terminals. It gracefully supported the title font, ensuring that it didn't overshadow the main focus while adding an element of intrigue.

For the body font, I chose Source Serif 4 Display Regular. Its lightweight design provided an excellent contrast to the thicker heading fonts, making the text easy to read and comprehend. This choice enhanced the overall readability of the cookbook, ensuring that readers could follow the recipe instructions seamlessly.

To further engage the audience, I integrated illustrations that related to the biryani-making process. These images not only added visual appeal but also served as helpful guides, enhancing the overall user experience. Through this typography project, I aimed to capture the essence of a generational recipe and present it in a way that celebrated its rich history and flavors, inviting others to embark on their own culinary adventures.
